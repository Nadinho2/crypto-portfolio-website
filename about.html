My Crypto Journey

Welcome to CryptoVault! I'm Nadinho, a passionate cryptocurrency enthusiast based in Lagos, Nigeria. With years of experience in the crypto market, I created this platform to simplify crypto education and portfolio tracking for beginners and intermediate traders.

My journey into crypto began in 2021 during the bull run. After experiencing both massive gains and painful losses, I realized how important proper education, risk management, and reliable information are in this volatile space.

What I Believe In

  • 💡 Education is the best investment in crypto
  • 🔒 Security and self-custody should always come first
  • 📊 Long-term thinking beats short-term hype
  • 🌍 Crypto should be accessible to everyone, including Africans
  • ⚖️ Never invest more than you can afford to lose

My Mission

CryptoVault was built with one goal in mind: to make crypto simple, safe, and accessible for Nigerians and beginners worldwide.

Through this website, I share honest guides on how and where to buy crypto, track trending coins, and provide practical knowledge that actually helps people make better decisions in the crypto market.
